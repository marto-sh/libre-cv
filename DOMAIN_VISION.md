# Domain Vision Statement

## For
> Tech professionals who want to own and share their professional identity, and anyone who wants to discover, evaluate, or interact with them.

## Who
> **Tech professionals** are tired of repeating the same information about themselves over and over again, and want a professional presence that works for them — engaging on their behalf and only escalating when it matters.
>
> **Those reaching out** struggle to assess fit from static profiles, and waste time on discovery interactions with people who aren't a match.

## The libre-cv is a
> Libre professional digital twin.

## That
> Lets you build a rich, structured representation of your professional self — your experience, skills, aspirations, and preferences — that works on your behalf. Others can discover and interact with your digital twin, which decides whether to escalate to you. It can also proactively seek opportunities for you.

## Unlike
> **LinkedIn**, which locks your professional identity behind a proprietary platform with no real data portability, and where your profile sits passively rather than working on your behalf.
>
> **Traditional CV tools** (Reactive Resume, FlowCV), which treat your profile as a formatted document rather than a living, interactable representation of who you are.
>
> **Jack & Jill AI**, which solves a similar problem with AI agents acting on behalf of job seekers and employers, but as a centralized, proprietary, commission-based service — you're a product in their network, not the owner of your data.
>
> **Traditional recruitment processes**, where both candidates and recruiters spend significant time on repetitive discovery interactions to assess basic fit.

## Our product
> Structures your professional identity as queryable, agent-friendly data rather than a formatted document, making it discoverable and interactable by both humans and AI agents. It's libre — you own both the code and your data.
