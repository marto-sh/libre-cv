> **Founded:** 2018
> **Founders:** Not publicly disclosed
> **Headquarters:** Estonia
> **Status:** Active

## Company Overview

FlowCV is a modern, proprietary resume builder that focuses on creating visually appealing resumes with minimal effort. Positioned as a "resume builder for the modern job seeker," FlowCV offers polished templates and an intuitive interface designed to help professionals create professional-looking resumes quickly.

The platform has gained popularity for its clean design aesthetic and user-friendly approach. While not open-source, FlowCV represents the contemporary approach to resume building in the commercial space.

## Product/Service Offering

### Core Features:

- **Modern Templates:** 20+ professionally designed resume templates
- **Intuitive Editor:** Drag-and-drop interface for easy customization
- **Content Suggestions:** AI-powered suggestions for resume content
- **ATS Optimization:** Templates designed to work with Applicant Tracking Systems
- **Cover Letter Builder:** Integrated cover letter creation tools
- **Multiple Export Formats:** PDF, Word, and plain text options
- **Cloud Storage:** Save and access resumes from anywhere
- **Multi-Language Support:** Support for multiple languages
- **Responsive Design:** Works on desktop and mobile devices

### Unique Aspects:

- **Design Focus:** Emphasis on visual appeal and modern aesthetics
- **Speed:** Quick resume creation process
- **ATS Compatibility:** Templates optimized for applicant tracking systems
- **User Experience:** Intuitive interface requiring minimal technical skill
- **Content Assistance:** Built-in suggestions for resume content

**USP:** "Create a beautiful resume in minutes" - FlowCV positions itself as the fastest way to create visually appealing, ATS-friendly resumes.

## Market Positioning

- **Limited Customization:** Templates have constraints on layout changes
- **No Data Portability:** Difficult to export data to other formats

- **No API Access:** No programmatic access to resume data

## Conclusion

FlowCV represents the current state-of-the-art in commercial resume building - polished, user-friendly, and visually appealing. However, it exemplifies the limitations of the traditional approach: **static documents created with proprietary tools that lock users into vendor-controlled ecosystems.**

The contrast highlights the fundamental shift libre-cv represents:
- **From:** "Create a beautiful resume" (FlowCV)
- **To:** "Empower your professional identity to work for you" (libre-cv)

This shift addresses the deeper need that professionals have - not just for better-looking resumes, but for **comprehensive, autonomous professional representation** that can actively advance their careers across multiple contexts.
